Tutorial 12: Econometric Input-Output-Model (IOM)

We consider the interplay between the power-plant sector producing electricity and heat and the mechanical engineering sector producing machines while all the other sectors are attributed to the end-consumer sector. When increasing the demand for machines, but not for energy, we will see that the production of both sectors must increase to re-attain the steady state
